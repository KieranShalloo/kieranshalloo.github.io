The Light Side

'A celebration of hope, heroism, and a galaxy far, far away — The Light Side is a lovingly illustrated tribute to the characters who fight for good. From wise old masters to rebel pilots and scrappy droids, this piece brings together the icons of the light side in a style that blends hand-drawn charm with digital screenprint textures.

The artwork began as a pencil and ink illustration, drawn by hand, before being coloured digitally using a layered technique that mimics traditional screenprinting. The result is bold and nostalgic — rich in texture, full of subtle details, and perfect for anyone who grew up dreaming of X-wings and lightsabers.

Printed at A3 size on beautifully smooth archival paper, it's ideal for hanging in a home office, studio, or alongside your collection of Star Wars prints, books or memorabilia.
