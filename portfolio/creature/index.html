Reduce Tech Debt

Advertising campaign for OutSystems

Brief

I was briefed for a short term project helping to create a website which aligned with an agency provided advertising campaign.

Agency

Creature London

Client

OutSystems

Project Date

2021

Role

Senior UX/UI Designer (contractor)

Web Design

The design agency Creature London brought me on board as a designer to help them with their project our OutSystems who were working on a low-code platform aimed at increasing their B2B reach.

This project involved liaising with the third party to ensure the branding and assets of the advertising campaign were front and centre to ensure consistency with the marketing message.

I created versions of the website and presented them back to the client, taking into account a mobile-first approach and worked iteratively on the design based on client feedback.
