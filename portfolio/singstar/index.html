Singstar

The PlayStation Music Videogame

Brief

I collaborated with the senior design team to help conceptualise future design patterns and in-game assets, in turn taking responsibility for designing production ready components.

I also worked closely with the motion graphics teams, assisting with the storyboarding of concepts and also making Lo-Fi mock ups to take further into the animatic stage.
