Our Process

How we handle our projects, from concept to delivery

Analytics

Understanding the behaviour of users allows us to find areas of improvement

Data Tracking

Manage trends and user behaviour over a long time, allowing us to tweak and adjust to user patterns

User Interviews

Identifying pain points of the existing experience with evidence to present to stakeholders

Competitor Reviews

Ensure to stay aware of competitors and the work they are producing

A/B Testing

Getting users to directly test designs allows us to see any pain points as early as possible.

Guerilla Testing

For quick design decisions, we take our testing to an informal real-life setting to quickly gather feedback from customers.

Iterative Design

Use feedback from testing sessions to guide the design process and steer the direction of projects

Task Based Tests

Allow participants results to provide evidence for design decisions

Animation

High quality After Effects animations to demonstrate micro-interactions

Wireframes

Mapping user journeys with wireframes to ensure great usability

Prototypes

Create prototypes to perfect the user journey and flow of designs

Visual Design

Production quality visual designs, bringing the wireframes to reality

Presentations

Giving feedback and updates to stakeholders about projects and deliverables

QA

Finding and raising bugs and issues that will negatively affect the user experience

Developer Checks

Working closely with development teams to ensure designs can be implemented correctly

User Acceptance Testing

Ensuring the live code accurately matches the Visual and Interaction design
