The Coastline

'The Coastline' is a hand-carved linocut relief print, designed and printed by hand inspired by the landscape of Portugal's rugged coastlibne. This 160 x 230mm lino print is hand printed on Canaletto Velino paper using traditional Black oil-based Cranfield "Caligo Safe Wash" relief ink.

This print is a limited edition of 30 and is signed, numbered, and dated in pencil.
